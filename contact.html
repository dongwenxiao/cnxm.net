Common Questions

Frequently Asked Questions

What is your minimum order quantity (MOQ)?

Our MOQ varies depending on the product type and customization requirements. For standard products, we typically require orders of 1000-5000 pieces. For custom solutions, please contact our sales team to discuss your specific needs.

What is the typical lead time for orders?

Lead times depend on order quantity and product complexity. Standard products usually ship within 2-4 weeks. Custom products require 6-8 weeks for tooling and initial production. We can provide expedited service for urgent orders.

Do you provide samples for testing?

Yes, we provide samples for qualified customers. Sample costs and shipping fees may apply, which can be credited toward your first production order. Contact us to request samples with your specifications.

What certifications do your products have?

Our products meet international standards including UL, CQC, RoHS, and REACH. Our facilities are certified to ISO 9001, ISO 14001, ISO 45001, and IATF 16949. Specific product certifications are available upon request.

Can you design custom connectors for our specific application?

Absolutely! We have a dedicated R&D team with extensive experience in custom connector design. Share your requirements with us, and we'll work with you to develop the perfect solution for your application.

What payment terms do you offer?

We accept various payment methods including T/T (Wire Transfer), L/C (Letter of Credit), and for established customers, we can discuss credit terms. Specific payment terms are negotiated based on order volume and customer relationship.
