Company Overview

18 Years of Excellence

Zhejiang Xuanmeng Electronics Co., Ltd. was founded in 2006 as a comprehensive solution provider in the connector industry. Over nearly two decades, we have established ourselves as a leading manufacturer specializing in the research, development, manufacturing, and sales of high-quality connectors and wire harness assemblies.

With a strong foundation built on innovation and quality, we have expanded our operations to include five wholly-owned subsidiaries:

  • Zhejiang Xuanmeng Electronic Technology Co., Ltd.
  • Guangdong Xuanmeng Electronic Technology Co., Ltd.

Advanced Production

Smart Manufacturing Excellence

Our commitment to technological advancement is reflected in our smart manufacturing capabilities. We have invested significantly in integrating advanced management systems to ensure precision, efficiency, and quality throughout our production process.

Integrated Systems:

  • ERP: Enterprise Resource Planning for streamlined operations
  • PLM: Product Lifecycle Management for innovative development
  • MES: Manufacturing Execution System for real-time control
  • Mold Management: Advanced tooling and mold design capabilities

These systems work together seamlessly to provide our clients with transparent, efficient, and high-quality manufacturing services from concept to delivery.
